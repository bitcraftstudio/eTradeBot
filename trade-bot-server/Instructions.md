This is a stock trading app that allows users to buy and sell stocks, view their portfolio, and track their performance.
This app also integrates with FMP "Financial Modeling Prep" to provide users with expert opinions and ratings on stocks, helping them make more informed decisions.
FMP documentation: https://site.financialmodelingprep.com/developer/docs
This app also integrates with xAI to provide recommendations of its own based on the data provided from FMP and its own analysis of the stock market.
This allows users to get a comprehensive view of the market and make more informed decisions when trading stocks.
One of the main goals is become full automated, allowing users to set up their trading strategies and let the app execute trades on their behalf based on those strategies.
